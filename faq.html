FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know before we start building your empire.

01

Getting Started

What services does AOURA offer for online sellers? +

We engineer e-commerce success on Amazon, NOON, and WooCommerce. Account setup, listing optimization, compliance handling, performance monitoring, and hands-on support—everything you need to launch fast and scale faster.

What do I need to get started? +

A valid UAE business license and a business bank account. Don't have them? We can guide you through the process—no guessing games.

What packages do you offer? +

Silver: The launchpad. Amazon + NOON setup.
Gold: For sellers ready to scale. Optimized listings + priority support.
Platinum: Full-stack management. We handle everything.
Custom: Multi-platform, high-volume, or unique needs—we build it.

Do your packages include support for multiple platforms? +

Yes. All packages support Amazon and NOON. Custom plans can add WooCommerce and WordPress website development. We ensure every account is compliant and optimized for maximum sales.

02

Pricing & Billing

Are there any hidden charges or extra fees? +

Zero. None. What you see is what you pay. Setup fee + monthly management fee. No surprise invoices. No "discovery fees." Transparency isn't a feature—it's our foundation.

Can I upgrade or change my plan later? +

Absolutely. Upgrade or switch anytime. Billing prorates based on your remaining period. Your growth shouldn't be limited by the package you picked on day one.

How do I make a payment? +

We accept all major debit/credit cards and PayPal. Payments are processed securely through Swipe—your financial data stays protected. Initial payment on signup, subsequent payments for ongoing services.

Are my payments secure? +

Ironclad. All payments go through Swipe, a trusted and encrypted payment gateway. Your card and account information never touches our systems.

03

Refunds & Cancellations

Can I get a refund if I change my mind? +

Yes—within 14 days if no work has started (no compliance checks, no system setup). Once we begin, a penalty may apply depending on how far we've progressed. Fair to you, fair to us.

What happens if work has already started and I request a refund? +

If compliance verification or system generation has begun, a penalty applies based on work completed. The remaining amount is refundable. We'll be transparent about exactly what was delivered.

Can I cancel my payment? +

Yes. Cancel anytime via our website, WhatsApp, or email. Work not started? Full refund. Work in progress? Penalty applies based on project stage. We don't hold your money hostage.
